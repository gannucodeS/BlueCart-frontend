Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?+
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also visit 'Your Orders' in your account and click 'Track Package' for real-time updates.
What is BlueCart's return policy?+
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Simply initiate a return from your account, drop the item at a courier point, and receive your refund within 5–7 business days.
Can I change or cancel my order?+
Yes — you can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, if the order has been dispatched, you will need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.
How long does delivery take?+
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (available in select cities) takes 1–2 days. Orders above ₹999 qualify for free standard shipping.
Is my payment information secure?+
Absolutely. BlueCart uses 256-bit SSL encryption and never stores full card details on our servers. We are PCI-DSS compliant and support UPI, net banking, cards & EMI.
How do I contact customer support?+
You can reach us via live chat (24/7), email at [email protected], or call 1800-000-BLUE (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–9 PM). Average response time is under 2 minutes.
